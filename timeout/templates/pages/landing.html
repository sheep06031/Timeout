Frequently Asked Questions

Timeout is a study productivity platform built for university students. It combines a Pomodoro focus timer, note-taking with categories, calendar and deadline management, a gamification system with XP and levels, statistics dashboards, direct messaging, and a social feed; all in one app so you can stay organized, focused, and connected with classmates.

You earn XP by creating notes (10 XP), editing notes (5 XP), completing Pomodoro focus sessions (25 XP), and maintaining daily study streaks (5 XP bonus per day). Your level is calculated from your total XP, and you can track your progress on the notes page and your profile.

Timeout offers light, dark, and system-default themes; colorblind-friendly modes for protanopia, deuteranopia, and tritanopia; and adjustable font sizes from 80% to 150%. You can also set your account to private to hide your posts from non-followers. All settings are available on the Settings page.

After signing up, you'll be guided through a profile completion step where you can set your university, year of study, and academic interests.
